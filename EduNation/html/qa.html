Q&A

What types of courses do you offer?

We offer a wide range of courses across multiple disciplines, including business, technology, healthcare, education, and more.

What is the duration of courses you offer?

The course duration varies depending on the course and its complexity. Some are self-paced and can be completed in a matter of weeks, while others may take several months.

What qualifications are required to enroll in your courses?

Our courses are designed to be accessible to anyone, regardless of their educational background or degree. But the prerequisites for each course vary based on subject matter.

Do you offer certificates or degrees upon completion of a course?

Yes, depending on the course, we offer completion certificates and diploma or degree programs that are nationally or regionally accredited. These programs are designed to help students move forward in their professional careers.

What are the instructional methods used in your courses?

Our courses use a combination of methods, such as online videos, interactive exercises, quizzes, and personalized assignments to interactive live sessions, expert analysis, and scenario-based learning to provide the best learning experience. We use both synchronous and asynchronous approaches to ensure that the material is delivered effectively to all students, regardless of their learning style.
